Payroll Management System

PMS is built based on groups, set of parameters, grades and categories. Salaries can be distributed based on cost centers and projects. Management of Vacations, attendances, loans and bonus are also available.Generating accounting vouchers to different accounts based on groups.

  • Accurate calculations
  • Compliance assistance
  • Access to tax forms
  • Flexible payments
  • Detailed recordkeeping
  • Employee self-service
